# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discrimination
- Abusive or offensive language
- Any form of misconduct

## Enforcement
Project maintainers have the right to remove or block contributors who violate this code.

By participating, you agree to follow these guidelines.
